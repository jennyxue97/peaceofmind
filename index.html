ABOUT US
TEAM
We are a team of two Computer Science undergraduates from MIT and one Economics undergraduate from Yale who are passionate about mental health awareness.
GOAL
Studies have shown that there is a direct correlation between workplaces that are accepting of mental health issues and have happier employees with better productivity.

Our goal is to promote mental health awareness in the workplace and provide information on how organizations can invest in the prevention of mental illnesses.
RESEARCH
Backed by data from the NIH, our model analyzes thousands of datapoints from employees across the United States while considering factors such as:
  • Whether if the company is in the tech field
  • Whether if the company provides mental health benefits
  • Whether if the company has a wellness program
  • The number of employees in the company
